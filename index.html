Guy named Pie

March 2018

There once was a guy named Pie.
Pie fell from the sky.
Pie's hair was green,
and once he was seen,
someone said he needs to die.

"He’s cursed,” said the big-nosed Blair.
“Sign of the slick, green hair—
he’ll turn our aunts
into cactus plants
’n’ sell them on that hill over there!”

Pie was chased out of town.
Upon his face was a frown.
A shotgun fired
and Pie perspired.
He heard, “Hey Pie! Get down!”

“You need to find a new place,”
said a guy with a shrunken face.
“Our town’s not far —
let’s go to the bar.
Your new home, soon, you’ll embrace”

From so much shock, Pie went lame.
“How d’you know my name?
You’re creepy as hell!
And I’d prefer to just, well,
dye my hair color of flame.”

Pie’s slick hair now looked red.
Nobody turned a head.
Pie thanked the guy
who lent the dye,
and then smiled going to bed.
